About Us

Sethmina Electricians is your premier partner for all bakery equipment needs. With over 10 years of experience, our team of seasoned technicians provides expert repair services for a wide range of bakery machinery.

We are dedicated to delivering reliable and efficient solutions, ensuring your business runs smoothly without any interruptions to your production.

Our Services

Bakery Equipment Repairs

We specialize in repairing all types of bakery machinery, including large dough mixers, gas ovens, diesel ovens, electric ovens, bun dividers, packing machines, rice steamers, and pastry sheeters.

Sales of Machinery & Spare Parts

We offer a comprehensive selection of both new and refurbished bakery machines and their genuine spare parts, ensuring long-lasting performance for your business.

Electrical Wiring & Repairs

Our expertise extends to professional electrical wiring and repair services for homes and commercial establishments, ensuring safety and reliability.

Service Area

We offer our services island-wide. No matter where you are located in Sri Lanka, our expert technicians are ready to assist you at your doorstep.
